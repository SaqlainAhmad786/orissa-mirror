Privacy Policy

At Orissa Mirror (orissamirror.com), your privacy is our priority.

We are committed to safeguarding the personal information of our readers, subscribers, and visitors while providing a transparent and secure browsing experience.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you visit our website or engage with our services.

By accessing or using orissamirror.com, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways — directly from you and automatically through your interaction with our website.

a) Information You Provide

When you register, subscribe, or participate in our services, we may request details such as:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Contact number
  • Gender, age, and postal PIN code
  • Occupation and interests
  • Payment details (for paid services)
  • Login credentials or passwords

This data helps us personalize your experience and deliver relevant news, alerts, and services.

b) Information We Collect Automatically

Our systems automatically collect certain technical details when you visit the site, such as:

  • IP address and device details
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and duration
  • Referring URL

This information helps us understand user behavior, improve our platform, and serve relevant content.

2. Use of Cookies

Orissa Mirror uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us:

  • Recognize returning users
  • Remember preferences
  • Analyze site performance and usage
  • Deliver relevant advertisements

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some sections of our site may not function properly without them.

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. Please note that we are not responsible for their privacy practices. Once you leave orissamirror.com, their policies apply.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Operate and maintain our website
  • Personalize your reading experience
  • Respond to your queries or feedback
  • Send newsletters, notifications, or promotional offers (when subscribed)
  • Process transactions or subscriptions
  • Prevent fraud and enhance site security
  • Comply with legal or regulatory requirements

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

However, your information may be shared in the following limited situations:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or court order, or to respond to lawful government requests.
  • Corporate Affiliates Within our organization or associated entities for operational or analytical purposes — always under strict confidentiality.
  • Third-Party Service Providers With trusted partners who help us operate our website or deliver services (e.g., payment processors, email systems, analytics). They are obligated to follow the same data protection standards as we do.

5. Accessing, Updating, or Deleting Your Information

You can request to:

  • Review your stored data
  • Update incorrect or outdated details
  • Delete your information (where legally possible)

To protect privacy, we may require identity verification before processing such requests. Certain information may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business reasons.

6. Information Security

We employ advanced security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure, including:

  • Secure servers protected by firewalls
  • Encrypted communication channels
  • Restricted access to internal systems

Despite our efforts, no online system is completely secure. We encourage users to exercise caution when sharing information online.

7. Advertising and Third-Party Links

We may use third-party advertising services that display ads relevant to you.

These partners may collect anonymized data (not including your name or contact details) about your browsing behavior across sites.

We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information.
